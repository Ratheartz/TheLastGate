The Original Creators of Astonia

Inventor, Lead Programmer, Artist, Tester and Web Site Author (retired)

Daniel Brockhaus alias Ishtar was born in '73 in Bremen/Germany. He moved around quite a bit and eventually found a home in Fischerhude, a small village close to Bremen.
Somehow he managed to finish school with the Abitur and began studying computer science at the University of Bremen. This turned out to be a bad choice. After leaving University, he worked for several years for a local Internet Provider. Then he got hired by the tallyman.de AG as Manager of Information Technology. After a year, tallyman was sold to START Ticket, and he was working there for half a year. Now he's spending his time writing a successor to Mercenaries.
