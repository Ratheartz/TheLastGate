Buglist for Mercenaries of Astonia v2.13.09

This list is up to date as of August, 2017. Bugs are listed in no particular order. Please do not report bugs already on this list.
  • Mine wall, when dug, has a grammatical error in the description.
  • A grave appearing just behind the entrance to the Arena is invisible, and a player may not know it's there to loot it.
  • Coins from the Black Stronghold do not age.
  • Victims of NPCs in the Random Dungeon do not bear the name of their killer.
  • Players walking through gates (in Arena and Lab 13) may end up in the same place as other characters, and become temporarily invisible.
  • When searching graves, the wrong grave might be displayed, or the display might jump from one grave to another.
  • Display of characters is not always correct when walking along (towards) a wall or over objects.
  • Some NPCs wait forever to be able to reach a certain place, thus blocking a place another NPC wants to go to. Creates a Deadlock...


All material on this server is based on the Mercenaries of Astonia engine by Daniel Brockhaus.
